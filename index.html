About Us

Trusted Healthcare
Since 2024

Nivali Dental Clinic was founded with one mission: to provide high-quality, affordable, and compassionate dental care to every patient in Nairobi. Our team of experienced dentists and dental professionals is committed to helping individuals and families achieve healthy, confident smiles every day.

From routine dental check-ups and professional cleanings to restorative and cosmetic treatments, we are equipped to care for your oral health needs with the warmth and personal attention that Kenyan families deserve.
